About Akrada

Akrada is a campus navigation platform built to help students find important locations around their university without getting lost.

It started as a simple idea — the struggle of finding lecture halls, departments, and offices in a new environment. A problem I lived. A problem I watched others live too.

Today, Akrada is growing campus by campus, powered by students and partners who believe campus life should be easier and more connected.

Built By

Akrada was built by Okami Nalado, a 100-level student in the Mathematics Department, majoring in Statistics at the University of Jos.

"To ensure that no student, anywhere, ever has to feel lost on campus again."

Vision

Akrada is not just an app.

It is a growing system for campus navigation — built for students, by a student, and expanding one campus at a time. 🌍🚀

Akrada is free for students. Always. Businesses pay to be discovered — never students.
